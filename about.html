The Bull'z Legacy

Our Origins

Established in 2015

In 2015, our journey began with nothing more than a shared family passion and a simple vision: to build stronger bodies and brighter futures right here in Carmen, Davao del Norte. We started small, rooted in the belief that fitness should be accessible, personal, and life-changing.

For ten years, we invested every bit of our energy—and our hearts—into a dream that many called "just a goal." In 2025, that dream finally took its full shape. Exactly a decade after our founding, we opened the doors to our brand-new facility, equipped with world-class machinery and a complete fitness experience.

Today, we are more than just a gym. We are a testament to what "investing and manifesting" can achieve. While our equipment is new, our humble roots remain the same. We are honored to inspire not only our neighbors in Carmen but fitness enthusiasts from across our neighboring cities. Come grow with us.
